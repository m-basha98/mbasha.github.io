About me

I am a PhD (student)researcher and developer from Canada with a strong foundation in NLP, Code Generation HCI, and the ethical and responsible deployment of AI. My work focuses on understanding and shaping how AI systems, including code generation tools, interact with people, organizations, and society at large.

I aim to create solutions that are both technically rigorous and socially responsible, balancing innovation with considerations of fairness, accountability, and transparency. From designing efficient, user-centered interfaces, to developing AI systems that are more trustworthy and inclusive, I approach every project with a commitment to impact and integrity. My research spans collaborations with academic institutions and contributions to applied AI research, ensuring that each endeavor advances both knowledge and practical value.

What I'm Doing

  • Generative AI in Software Engineering

    Conducting research on generative AI in software engineering, focusing on code generation tools like GitHub Copilot and OS models like CodeBERT. My work addresses fairness such as implicit biases and aims to improve the trustworthiness of AI-generated code by developing frameworks, pipelines, and tools, alongside analysis and user-centered studies.

  • User Behavior Insights

    Transforming complex social and behavioral data into actionable insights to inform product strategy for code generation, optimize user engagement by discovering/addressing user pain points, and support evidence-based decision-making.

  • AI-Powered Product Development

    Designing and evaluating AI-driven software solutions that improve user workflows, integrate responsible AI practices, and ensure scalable, trustworthy adoption of generative AI technologies.

  • STEM Outreach & Mentorship

    Leading initiatives to inspire underrepresented groups in STEM by organizing events, mentoring students, and speaking on academic and professional pathways in AI and technology.
